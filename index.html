Work Day Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day

Instructions

Use this application to schedule your work day. Type in your task and click the save button. If you want to clear your tasks, click the "clear tasks" button at the bottom

  • Gray: past
  • Blue: present
  • Green: future
